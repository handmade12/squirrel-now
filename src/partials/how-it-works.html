How it works?

Select a Host

You will select a Host within your neighborhood
accept and secure your packages.

Send to Host

You will send your packages to your
Host so they can secure them in their home.

Storing

Your Host will store your package for as
long as you need them to. 24 hours to months!

Finish

You can pick-up your package, have it
delivered or stored for a period of time. Your Choice!
